# M Articles

A growing collection of long-form articles spanning technology, psychology, and everything in between — written to inform, not to impress.

---

## About

M Articles is an open publication covering the things most people use, experience, or live through — without ever stopping to understand. Each article is researched, structured, and written with one goal: to leave the reader genuinely knowing something they did not before.

No paywalls. No noise. Just writing worth reading.

---

## Topics

The publication is not confined to a single field. Articles may cover:

- **Technology** — formats, systems, tools, and the decisions behind them
- **Psychology** — how the mind works, why people behave the way they do
- **Society & Culture** — patterns, ideas, and the forces that shape everyday life
- **And more** — wherever curiosity leads
